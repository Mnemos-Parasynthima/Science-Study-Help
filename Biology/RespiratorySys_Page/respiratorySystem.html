Functions

The responsibility of the respiratory system is to provide oxygen to the tissues in the body and get rid of carbon dioxide. Without it, oxygen wouldn't get to our cells, the cells would stop working and we could probably die. Which is why it's very pivotal to all of us. It does it via the circulatory system.
